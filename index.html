A little about me

Led and coordinate a tech team (front & back) to apply and develop the design principle of each project. Previously I worked with company like Decathlon, Estrella Damm and PepsiCo. on commercial campaigns.

I graduated from ILAS - Accademia Italiana di Comunicazione Visiva with a degree in Graphic and Web Design. In the following years, I specialise in design of digital product. My main goal is to help people build better products.

My work ranges from strategic planning and rough prototyping in early stages to the creation of a visual design language that increases both the brand and the product value
