Decathlon

UI & UX Design
Challenge

Decathlon is a french brand that collected a string of successes for years. Its activity begins in 1976 in France, expanding itself up to be present in 30 countries, thus becoming a multinational company.

One of its slogans is "el deporte màs grande del mundo". Bringing together, in fact, under its main brand a number of small brands, its necessity is to differentiate them supplying, to each brand and to each marketing campaign, their own visual identity.

Our mission, has precisely been this one: give Decathlon a diversified image -for its brands- but still coherent -with its original values.-
