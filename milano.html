Milano Bar & Trattoria is a little corner of Italy in the center of Barcelona. When you come to Milano you can take a delicious Aperol spritz with an surprising and fascinating soundtrack by Donatella Rettore and Raffaella Carrá. The redesign of Milano was a complex project leaded by one of the most important studio of restaurant interior design. Working with Steef, the creative director of NE-ON Studio, has been a great experience and amazing opportunity to grow up as designer.
